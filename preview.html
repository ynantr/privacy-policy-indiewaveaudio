Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how IndieWave Audio collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile applications focused on music, audio, rhythm, and virtual instruments.

Effective Date: April 3, 2026

IndieWave Audio ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes mobile applications related to music, audio, and instrument experiences. By using our apps, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your full name, email address, phone number, or home address unless you voluntarily provide it, for example when contacting support.

b. Non-Personal Information

We may automatically collect certain non-personal information, including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • General region, country, and language settings

c. Audio and Microphone Data

Some of our apps may request access to your device microphone to enable features such as audio recording or interactive instrument playback.

  • Audio is processed locally on your device
  • We do not store, upload, or share your recordings unless clearly stated otherwise in a specific app

d. Storage Access

We may request access to device storage to:

  • Save recordings created by you
  • Load audio files used inside the app

Files remain on your device unless you choose to export or share them yourself.

e. Advertising Data

Our apps may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device identifiers
  • Ad interactions and performance data

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve app performance, quality, and stability
  • Understand usage trends and feature engagement
  • Deliver and measure advertising
  • Enhance the overall user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools and platforms, including:

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • Google Analytics for Firebase for analytics and diagnostics

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited non-personal or aggregated data with service providers and analytics partners for app improvement, diagnostics, and advertising operations.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights related to your information, such as the right to request access, deletion, or restriction of certain data processing. You may also limit ad tracking by adjusting your device privacy settings or resetting your advertising identifier.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
